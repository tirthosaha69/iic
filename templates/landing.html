How It Works

From satellite to your screen in seconds

01
Live Weather Fetch
OpenWeatherMap API delivers current temperature, rainfall, humidity, wind, and air quality index.
02
Risk Computation
Flask backend applies hydrology rules: if 3h rain > 20mm → flood HIGH. AQI ≥ 4 → pollution HIGH.
03
AI Interpretation
Groq LLM receives all live values, interprets conditions, and generates location-specific alerts.
04
Live Dashboard
Browser renders all data on the interactive map with 32,655 real West Bengal water features.
